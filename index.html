About FixMyStreet

FixMyStreet is a civic engagement platform that empowers citizens to report and track local issues in their communities.

How it works

  1. Report an issue with a photo and location
  2. Your report is sent to local authorities
  3. Track progress and earn points
  4. See all issues on the interactive map
